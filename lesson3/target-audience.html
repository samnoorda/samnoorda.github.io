Target Audience

The target audience for 'The Temperature for Travelors' will be those who have reason to travel to various parts of World. Weather is an important part of planning excursions so that our patrons know what to wear and what to pack. I want this website to be a reliable place that people can go to plan their trips

Personas

Caleb is a 25-year-old male with no kids. He and his wife Kim are always looking for their next adventure. They enjoy the time that they get to spend traveling together because they feel it brings them closer together.
Chris is a 37-year-old man who is in the auditing profession. His job requires him to travel to clients' locations all over the pacific northwest. Because he is often at this location for weeks, he wants to make sure that he packs correctly.

Scenarios

1. Caleb and Kim are interested to travel to some remote destination in Idaho to go hiking and hammocking.
2. Chris is asked to travel to Preston to audit ABC company's books.
3. Caleb is invited to attend his high school reunion and wants to pack accordingly.
4. Chris has a weekend off and wants to visit other parts of Idaho.
5. Caleb and Kim are interesting in doing a photoshoot with a beautiful nature backdrop and want to check the weather first.
